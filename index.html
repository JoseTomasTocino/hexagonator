Hexagonator generates abstract wallpapers made out of hexagons based on the image you choose. Fill your minimalism needs with this powerful, cutting-edge web application that surprisingly does not use React, jQuery, NodeJS or Gulp!

Select the hexagon size, the softness of the color transitions and the input file, and click generate.

To download the wallpaper, right click on it and select Save image as.
